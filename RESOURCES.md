## KEY PRINCIPLES FOR USING RESOURCES

**Don't binge watch.** 1 video per slot. Take notes. Code the example. Then move on.

**Watch AFTER attempting.** For DSA: try for 30 min → if stuck → watch codestorywithMIK/Striver. Don't watch first.

**PP is your Java foundation.** 1 lesson per day in morning block. Not 3. Not 5. 1. Deep, not wide.

**Hello Interview is your SD gym.** One mock per week from Week 4. Consistency beats cramming.

**Striver A2Z sheet is your DSA spine.** Follow its order. It's optimised for interview frequency. Don't skip sections.

**Arpit Bhayani is your depth.** His microservices videos are production-grade. Read at 1AM when the day's work is done and your brain is in absorption mode.
